Hi! This is my Pong game created by Thuan Nguyen.

Controls:
Player 1 - W to move up, S to move down

Player 2 - Up Arrow key to move up, Down Arrow key to move down

Mode:
Player vs Player - 2 players controls

Player vs AI - 1 Player control (W and S)

Mechanics:
- Ball Movement and Bounce
- Player Paddle Controls
- Player vs Player and Player vs AI
- Scoring System
- UI: Main Menu and Win Screen 
- Sounds and Music
- Visual Effects: post processing glowing, ball trail and collision particles.


Thanks for playing!
